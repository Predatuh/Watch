# Cute Notes — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

Cute Notes is a watch app for sending little animated notes to friends. This
policy explains what it collects and why. It is intentionally minimal because
the app is intentionally minimal.

## What we collect

- **An anonymous account ID.** When you open the app it creates an anonymous
  Firebase account. It is not tied to your name, email, phone number, or Google
  account.
- **Your username.** The username you choose so friends can add you.
- **Your friends list.** The usernames/IDs of people you add as friends.
- **Notes you send.** When you send a note (an expression, a firework, or a
  drawing), it is stored briefly so it can be delivered to the recipient, then
  **deleted from the server once their device receives it.**

We do **not** collect your name, email, contacts, location, or any analytics or
advertising identifiers. There are no ads. We do not sell or share your data.

## Where data is stored

Data is stored using **Google Firebase** (Firestore and Anonymous Authentication).
Google processes it on our behalf; see Google's privacy policy at
https://policies.google.com/privacy.

## Data retention

- Notes are deleted from the server as soon as they are delivered.
- Your username and friends list remain until you delete them or request account
  deletion.

## Deleting your data

To delete your account and data, contact us at the email below and we will remove
your username, friends list, and any pending notes.

## Children

Cute Notes is not directed to children under 13.
